About InExTracker

InExTracker app helps you stay on top of your finances by allowing you to easily track your income and expenses. With our app, you can easily add and categorize your transactions, set budgets, and view detailed reports to gain insights into your spending habits. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to stay organized and reach your financial goals. Whether you're trying to save money, pay off debt, or just want to better understand your spending, our app is the perfect solution. Download now and take control of your finances today!

Features

Organization

Our app allows you to easily input and categorize your financial transactions, giving you a clear overview of where your money is going.

Detailed Analysis

Get insights into your spending habits with detailed reports and charts. Understand where you're over or under budget, and make adjustments accordingly.

Multi Currency

Manage and track your finances in multiple currencies, and automatically convert to your local currency. Perfect for travelers or people who have income/expenses in different currencies..

Cross Platform

Access your finances on the go or at home with our mobile and web-based app.
